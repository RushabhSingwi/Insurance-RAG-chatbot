Re: Treatment of inward Co - Insurance while reporting MTP Obligations 1. , 1938 , 2015 
1.Section 32D of Insurance Act, 1938 specifies that every insurer carrying on
general insurance business shall, after the commencement of the Insurance
Laws (Amendment) Act, 2015, underwrite such minimum percentage of
insurance business in third party risks of motor vehicles as may be specified by
the regulations.
2. Accordingly, the obligations of an Insurer in respect of Motor Third Party
Insurance Business for every Financial Year are also specified in IRDAI
(Obligation of Insurer in Respect of Motor Third Party Insurance Business)
Regulations, 2015.
3. Where insurers undertake Co-insurance arrangement as part of risk sharing
program, it is clarified that the inward co-insurance shall not be considered for the
purpose of reckoning the compliance to the above referred regulations.
4. This has the approval of the competent authority.
