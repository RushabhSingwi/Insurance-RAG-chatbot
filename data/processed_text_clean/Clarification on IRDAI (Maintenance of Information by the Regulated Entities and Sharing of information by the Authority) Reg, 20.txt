To all Foreign Reinsurer's branches and Lloyd's India (including its Syndicates and service
companies)
Sub: Clarification on IRDAI (Maintenance of Information by the Regulated
Entities and Sharing of information by the Authority) Regulations, 2025 
By virtue of the powers conferred under Regulation 17 of IRDAI (Maintenance of
Information by the Regulated Entities and Sharing of Information by the Authority),
Regulations 2025, the following clarification is issued:
With reference to the proviso to the regulation 9 of the aforesaid regulations, it is clarified
that Foreign Reinsurer's branches and Lloyd's India (including its Syndicates and service
companies) when they collect such records, those records shall be maintained in India.
This is applicable to every record including those maintained in electronic form.
It is further clarified that FRBs and Lloyd's India (including its Syndicates and Service
companies) shall comply with all the provisions stipulated under IRDAI (Maintenance of
Information by the Regulated Entities and Sharing of Information by the Authority),
Regulations 2025
