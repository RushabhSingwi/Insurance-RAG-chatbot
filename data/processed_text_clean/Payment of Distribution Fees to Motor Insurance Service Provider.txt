To,
ALL GENERAL INSURERS CARRYING ON MOTOR INSURANCE BUSINESS

Re: Payment of Distribution Fees to Motor Insurance Service Provider 

1. Attention is drawn to Para 15(5) in Chapter V of Guidelines on Motor Insurance
Service Provider (MISP) vide Ref No. IRDA/INT/GDL/MISP/202/08/2017 dated
31st August, 2017 on payment of distribution fees to the MISP directly by the
insurer or by the insurance intermediary as the case may be. 
2. Consequent upon notification of IRDAI (Expenses of Management of Insurers
transacting General or Health Insurance Business) Regulations, 2023 dated
26t March, 2023 and IRDAI (Payment of Commission) Regulations, 2023 dated
26" March, 2023, the Para 15 (5) (c) on the maximum distribution fees payable
to MISP is replaced as under: “The distribution fees payable to MISP shall be as per Board approved policy for
payment of commission of the insurer.” 
3. The provisions in all the extant circulars relating to distribution fees payable to
MISP are hereby withdrawn. However, all other relevant provisions relating to
MISP shall continue to remain in effect. 
4. This is issued with the approval of the competent authority. 
CC: The Secretary General, General Insurance Council, Mumbai
